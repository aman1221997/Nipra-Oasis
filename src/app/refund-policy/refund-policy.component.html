Refund Policy

Last Updated: December 15, 2024

1. Overview

At Nipra Oasis, we strive to provide the best quality products and services. This refund policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds may be issued for our water supply, pizza delivery, and gas cylinder services.

2. Water Supply Refund Policy

2.1 Quality Issues

If you receive water that does not meet our quality standards:

  • Report the issue within 24 hours of delivery
  • Provide photographic evidence if possible
  • We will replace the jar free of charge or provide a full refund

2.2 Damaged or Leaking Jars

  • Report damaged or leaking jars immediately upon delivery
  • We will replace the jar at no additional cost
  • No refund will be issued if damage occurs after delivery acceptance

2.3 Subscription Cancellations

  • Daily/Weekly Plans: Can be cancelled anytime with 24 hours notice. Refund for unused deliveries will be processed within 7 business days
  • Monthly Plans: Cancellation allowed within first 7 days for full refund. After 7 days, prorated refund for unused portion
  • Yearly Plans: Cancellation within 30 days for 80% refund. After 30 days, prorated refund minus 20% processing fee

3. Pizza Delivery Refund Policy

3.1 Quality Issues

If your pizza does not meet quality standards:

  • Report within 30 minutes of delivery
  • Provide photos of the issue
  • We will offer a replacement pizza or full refund

3.2 Wrong Order

  • If you receive the wrong pizza, contact us immediately
  • We will deliver the correct order at no charge
  • Full refund available if replacement is not desired

3.3 Delivery Delays

  • If delivery exceeds 60 minutes from order placement, you may request a refund
  • Delays due to weather or unforeseen circumstances may not qualify for refund
  • We will offer a discount on your next order as compensation

3.4 Order Cancellation

  • Orders can be cancelled within 5 minutes of placement for full refund
  • After 5 minutes, if preparation has started, no refund will be issued
  • If pizza is already out for delivery, cancellation is not possible

4. Gas Cylinder Refund Policy

4.1 Damaged or Defective Cylinders

  • Inspect cylinder upon delivery and report any damage immediately
  • Defective cylinders will be replaced free of charge
  • Safety seals must be intact for replacement eligibility

4.2 Wrong Cylinder Type

  • If you receive the wrong cylinder type, contact us immediately
  • We will exchange it for the correct type at no additional cost
  • Full refund available if exchange is not possible

4.3 Order Cancellation

  • Orders can be cancelled before dispatch for full refund
  • Once cylinder is dispatched, cancellation is not possible due to safety regulations
  • Refund processing time: 7-10 business days

4.4 Non-Refundable Situations

  • Cylinders that have been used or tampered with
  • Damage caused by customer negligence
  • Cylinders accepted without inspection

5. Refund Process

5.1 How to Request a Refund

  1. Contact our customer service via phone or email
  2. Provide your order number and details of the issue
  3. Submit any required evidence (photos, etc.)
  4. Our team will review your request within 24-48 hours

5.2 Refund Methods

  • Original payment method (for online payments)
  • Bank transfer (provide account details)
  • Store credit for future purchases

5.3 Refund Timeline

  • Approval notification: Within 48 hours
  • Refund processing: 5-7 business days
  • Bank credit: Additional 3-5 business days depending on your bank

6. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Products consumed or used
  • Services already rendered
  • Promotional or discounted items (unless defective)
  • Delivery charges (unless delivery failed due to our error)

7. Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be granted in cases where:

  • Only part of the order is affected
  • Service was partially completed
  • Customer accepts a partial resolution

8. Exchanges

We offer exchanges for:

  • Wrong products delivered
  • Defective items
  • Damaged goods (reported immediately)

Exchanges are subject to product availability and must be requested within the specified timeframe for each service.

9. Force Majeure

Refunds may not be available for delays or cancellations caused by events beyond our control, including but not limited to:

  • Natural disasters
  • Government restrictions
  • Strikes or labor disputes
  • Severe weather conditions

In such cases, we will work with you to reschedule delivery or provide alternative solutions.

12. Dispute Resolution

If you are not satisfied with our refund decision, you may:

  • Request escalation to a senior manager
  • Provide additional evidence for reconsideration
  • Seek resolution through consumer protection forums

We are committed to fair and transparent resolution of all refund requests.
